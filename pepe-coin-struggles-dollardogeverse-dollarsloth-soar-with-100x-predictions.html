Pepe coin struggles, $DOGEVERSE, $SLOTH soar with 100x predictions

Publish date: 2024-09-01

While Pepe Coin's recent price struggles have cast doubt on its ability to reach new all-time highs (ATH) in 2024, new Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) from Dogeverse and Slothana are generating buzz with their innovative features and ambitious roadmaps. Analysts are predicting explosive growth for these new meme coins, with some estimating a potential 100x increase in value.

Pepe Coin: Bullish Pattern Hints at Potential Summer Surge

Pepe Coin, the meme coin inspired by the internet frog of the same name, has captivated investors with its meteoric rise. After surpassing a market capitalisation of $2.2bn in April 2024, PEPE entered a bullish pattern, raising hopes of a price surge towards its ATH of $0.00001.

Analysts like Crypto Tony identified a symmetrical triangle pattern on PEPE's chart, suggesting a potential breakout if the price surpasses $0.00001. This breakout could propel PEPE to new highs, with some analysts eyeing targets as high as $0.000009.

However, concerns remain regarding PEPE's ability to sustain a bull run. A recent market crash caused PEPE's price to plummet by over 11%, highlighting the meme coin's vulnerability to broader market swings.

Additionally, on-chain metrics revealed increasing selling pressure, with whales offloading their PEPE holdings and the supply of PEPE on exchanges rising. These factors could hinder PEPE's short-term growth.

Dogeverse: A Multi-Chain Meme Coin Primed for Take-off?

Dogeverse emerges as a strong contender in the meme coin race, offering a unique selling proposition – multi-chain compatibility.

ADVERTISEMENT

Unlike most meme coins confined to a single blockchain like Ethereum, Dogeverse aims to be available on a multitude of blockchains, including Ethereum (ETH), BNB Chain (BNB), Polygon (MATIC), Solana (SOL), Avalanche (AVAX) and Base (BASE).

This multi-chain approach caters to users with varying preferences for transaction fees, network speeds and functionality. For instance, users seeking faster transactions and lower fees might opt for the Solana or Polygon blockchains, while those prioritising security might choose Ethereum.

Beyond its multi-chain accessibility, Dogeverse boasts a staking protocol, allowing $DOGEVERSE token holders to earn passive rewards.

The project hints at future development plans that extend beyond pure meme status, potentially venturing into areas like NFTs and DeFi. This blend of features and a loyal Dogecoin (DOGE) fanbase positions Dogeverse as a project to watch in the coming months.

Popular YouTubers ClayBro and Austin Hilton have endorsed Dogeverse, highlighting its potential for significant growth.

Slothana: A Laid-Back Contender with 100x Potential?

Slothana ($SLOTH), a newcomer to the meme coin scene, has captured the imagination of investors with its laid-back charm and strategic launch date.

The red-eyed sloth mascot embodies the spirit of April 20th, a day synonymous with meme culture and the "4/20" celebration within the crypto community. This strategic launch strategically coincides with Bitcoin's halving event, further fueling investor excitement.

ADVERTISEMENT

Capitalising on the recent success of Solana meme coins and the hype surrounding the Bitcoin halving, Slothana has garnered significant interest during its presale.

The project strategically implemented a limited-time presale window, fostering a sense of urgency and exclusivity. This approach, coupled with endorsements from prominent crypto analysts like Jacob Bury and ClayBro who predict a potential 100x increase in value, has fueled investor enthusiasm.

Slothana's limited presale window and fixed token price could contribute to a price surge post-launch. The project has already surpassed its presale target by a staggering $10 million, exceeding the presale figures of other leading Solana meme coins. This overwhelming investor interest is a strong indicator of Slothana's potential. With the presale window nearing its close, those interested in acquiring $SLOTH tokens are encouraged to act swiftly.

The meme coin market remains unpredictable yet brimming with potential. While established players like Pepe Coin grapple with short-term volatility, innovative new projects like Dogeverse and Slothana are carving their own paths.
