Social Self Portraits: Olympic Athletes Best Photos From London 2012

Publish date: 2024-08-15

The member of the gold medal winning US team and silver-medal vault gymnast made waves when she displayed her now infamous scowl on the Olympic podium. Her expression prompted scores of criticism, as well as a hugely popular meme called “McKayla Is Not Impressed.” But on August 12, she and a few of her teammates joined in on the joke with a photo McKayla posted to Instagram and Twitter. Boasting the familiar frown, the ladies announced that the pool was closed and they were #notimpressed.
