Fameye - TGIF ft. Dj Mic Smith: video, lyrics, reactions

Publish date: 2024-07-08

The much-awaited track by Fameye - TGIF featuring Dj Mic Smith is out. The song translates to 'Thank God Its Friday' and expresses what most people pray for. Most workers look forward to Friday as it ushers in the weekend where they get to rest and chill after a week of working hard. This banger is a definite hit and a potential household anthem.

Do you enjoy dancing? The song's beats are very catchy and addictive. It will not be surprising if you keep hitting the replay button as you dance to the tune.

Fameye - TGIF description

  • Release date: 8th November 2019
  • Format: Video
  • Genre: Highlife-Afrobeat fusion
  • Length: 3:48 minutes
  • Producer: B2

It is true that no one knows tomorrow. We should, therefore, have a good time and enjoy what life has to offer. The track comes together beautifully from the lyrics to the visuals. You will enjoy!
