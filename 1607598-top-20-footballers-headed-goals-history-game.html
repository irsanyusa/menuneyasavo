Top 20 footballers with the most headed goals in the history of the game

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Soccer players use different techniques to score goals. Some of the greatest goal-scoring styles are the snap header, the diving header, the chipped/lobbed effort, the backheel strike, the bicycle kick/volleyed effort, the curled effort, the howitzer wonder strike, the free kick, the penalty, chest, left/right foot strike, and the simple tap-in. Since players often try to impress fans with their header skills, this article unveils 20 famous footballers who have scored the most headed goals ever.

Players love the header techniques because these goals are often harder for goalkeepers to save. As a result, over 700 goals scored in the world's top five soccer leagues are headers, and on average, one in every five goals are headers. Christiano Ronaldo, Luuk De Jong, and Robert Lewandowski have been hailed as the undisputed kings of football headers. You can find out how many headed goals have scored and more players who have attained an impressive number of the headed goals in the world.
