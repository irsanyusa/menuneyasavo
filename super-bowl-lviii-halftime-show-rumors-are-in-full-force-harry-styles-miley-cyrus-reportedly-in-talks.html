The wait for the next Super Bowl Halftime Performer is far from over. Even though the game is nearly 8 months away, rumors have begun about the Super Bowl LVIII halftime show. Initially, these rumors revolved around Harry Styles as a potential performer, but another pop icon has officially joined the game. According to recent rumors, Miley Cyrus may be in the talks as Super Bowl LVIII’s halftime show performer.

Miley Cyrus is Reportedly in Talks For The Super Bowl Halftime Show

Miley Cyrus is the latest name to be linked to the 2024 Super Bowl Halftime Show. On June 12, Pop culture news updates channel, Pop Tingz, reported that Cyrus is in talks to perform in the Super Bowl Halftime Show. This would be a major coup for the NFL, as Cyrus is one of the most popular and successful singers in the world.

If Cyrus is chosen to perform at the Super Bowl, she would be following in the footsteps of some of the biggest names in music. Past performers have included Beyoncé, Lady Gaga, Coldplay, and Katy Perry. The Super Bowl Halftime Show has become one of the most watched events on television, and it is a huge opportunity for any artist.

Aside from Cyrus, Harry Styles has also been a rumored potential performer. For those who may not remember, Styles was apart of the original X Factor UK group, One Direction, who quickly rose to fame after the series. Now, he has launched a successful solo career, winning numerous awards, including a Grammy Award.

In addition, the singer has shown interest in performing at the event in the past. Although Styles’ solo career is already extremely successful, this would be a major moment for him. The global pop star would have the ability to show off his skills on an arguably larger scale.

What to Expect From a Miley Cyrus or Harry Styles Super Bowl Halftime Show

If Cyrus or Styles are indeed chosen to perform at the Super Bowl Halftime Show, fans can expect a high-energy and entertaining performance. Both artists are known for their charismatic stage presence and their ability to connect with audiences.

Cyrus is known for her wild and energetic live shows. It’s no secret that she’s not afraid to push the boundaries and experiment with different styles. Her performances are always full of surprises, and she is always sure to put on a show. This move would make total sense for Cyrus, since she already performs once a year on a national level. For those who may have missed it, she’s hosted Miley’s New Year’s Eve Party on NBC for the past two years. Within the special, Cyrus is a host and also a performer.

In recent years, Styles has earned a similar reputation for his live shows. He’s known as a talented dancer and singer, that always puts on a high-quality performance. His shows are always fun and engaging, and he always leaves his fans wanting more.

Both Cyrus and Styles have a large and dedicated fan base. If either of them is chosen to perform at the Super Bowl, it’s sure to be a ratings success. The show would be watched by millions of people around the world, and it would be a major moment for both artists.
