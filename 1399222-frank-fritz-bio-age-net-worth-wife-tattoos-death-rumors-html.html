Career summary

Fritz worked in Iowa as a fire and safety inspector for many years before giving it up to fully follow his passion for finding treasures in antiques and vintage items.

After he bought one item for $15 and sold it for $475, he realized he could make a living from such trade.

He founded the Antique Archeology shop in Le Claire, Iowa, with his friend and business partner Mike Wolfe. Fritz and Wolfe have travelled to over 200 cities in the USA, collecting and refurbishing antiques and vintage items and selling them.
