Lil Durk (Rapper and Singer)

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Full Name: Durk Derrick Banks
Profession: Rapper and Singer

Biography: Lil Durk, born Durk Derrick Banks, is an American rapper and singer. He first came to fame with his mixtape series called Signed to the Streets (2013-2014), which led to him receiving a label deal with Def Jam Recordings.

Durk's debut studio album Remember My Name (2015) was released with Def Jam, as was his subsequent album Lil Durk 2X (2016). He then returned to the mixtape game, releasing Cause Y'all Waited (2018) before being signed by Alamo Records.

His first Billboard Hot 100 single was with 'Viral Moment' in 2020, which was a song from Just Cause Y'all Waited 2 (2020). Since then he has continued to have commercial success with a number of hit singles.
