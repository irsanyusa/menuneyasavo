Celine Dion Is Turning 55, and Shes Sharing Her Instant Facelift and Other Tips for Ageless

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Despite being in her fifties, Celine Dion still looks ageless. She embraces her natural beauty and doesn’t try to hide her age. She loves her wrinkles and gray hair, and she still looks fresh and youthful thanks to her simple skincare routine and a positive mindset.

She has a very simple skincare routine.

Following a complicated beauty routine isn’t always the best when caring for your skin, and the “Falling Into You” singer likes to keep it simple. She cleanses and hydrates her skin and keeps her brushes clean to prevent bacteria from getting onto her skin. “Clean face, moisturize, and keep your products that you use clean,” she said.

She uses oil-based makeup.

From hydrating your skin to preventing annoying breakouts, oils have lots of benefits for your skin. Many celebrities prefer olive oil over expensive skincare products to maintain their youthful looks. Dion believes using oil-based makeup can benefit your skin in many ways. “Choose oil-based formulas. If it’s water-based and I tear up, it will streak. If it’s oil-based, it doesn’t budge,” she shared.

She has a needle-free facelift hack.

More and more celebrities speak out against plastic surgery, and some openly share why they regret getting fillers or Botox. Dion has a smart trick up her sleeve that helps her instantly get a refreshed look. “I would wear my hair up in a chignon or up on top of my head, so it kind of gives you a little lift in a way. So it makes you just like, up,” she shared.

She believes beauty is about more than just looks.

A big believer in aging gracefully, Dion shared her views on beauty. “Maybe you’re not out there on stage or out there on TV, but we all have a voice. Because we are worth it. We have dreams. Feeling beautiful makes you feel strong. And feeling strong makes you succeed,” she said.
