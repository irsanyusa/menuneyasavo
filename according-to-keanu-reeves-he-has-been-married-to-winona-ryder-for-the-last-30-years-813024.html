According to Keanu Reeves, He Has Been Married to Winona Ryder for the Last 30 Years

Publish date: 2024-07-09

From their on-screen chemistry to their off-screen friendship, these 2 have captured our hearts time and time again. And although this ultimate power couple never dated, they recently worked out that they’ve been married for 30 years. Now, 3 decades later, the pair are still good friends and joke around about being married. Discover how they accidentally got married in real life, making our dream ’90s couple a reality.

They have been close for decades.

We all know that Keanu is the gentleman of Hollywood, and a huge heartthrob, who has been appearing on our screens for decades as one lovable character or another. However, a lesser-known fact is that he is actually married to none other than superstar and ’90s icon Winona Ryder. They have been known to be close friends for years now and have starred in several movies together, including the 1992 Bram Stoker’s Dracula, where they accidentally got legally married.

Their marriage

As Winona reveals in an interview, during the wedding scene in Dracula back in 1992, they got married in real life: “We actually got married in Dracula... No, I swear to god, I think we’re married in real life. In that scene, Francis used a real Romanian priest. We shot the master, and he did the whole thing. So, I think we’re married.”

Keanu later verifies the story in an interview, confirming that they are, in fact, husband and wife to this very day. He said: “We did a whole take of a marriage ceremony with real priests... Winona says we are [married]. Coppola says we are. So I guess we’re married... under the eyes of God.”

They’ve had a “big, healthy” crush on each other.

Jordan Strauss/Invision/AP/East News

Being married to each other for the last 30 years “under the eyes of God” hasn’t stopped them from falling in love with other people. Keanu has been together with Alexandra Grant, who he is currently gushing about, since at least 2019. Winona has also been in a serious relationship since 2011 when she started dating Scott Mackinlay Hahn.

They have both also admitted that they’ve had “big, healthy” crushes on each other in the past, but nothing has ever come of it. Let’s not hold our breath for it to happen, as they both seem very happy in their current relationships.

Keanu Reeves is known to be private about his personal life, but what we have seen of him with his long-term girlfriend, Alexandra Grant, gives us warm, happy feelings. He deserves happiness, and they really seem to be very happy and very much in love with each other.
