Fred Again Announces Places Weve Never Been North American Tour

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Fresh off of announcing his new album “Ten Days,” Fred Again has revealed the dates for his upcoming Places We’ve Never Been tour, slated to make its way around North America this fall.

The 14-date tour kicks off on September 11 with two scheduled dates at Ball Arena in Denver. Some of the trek features multiple shows in the same cities — Seattle, East Troy and Toronto — as well as one-offs in Minneapolis, Buffalo and Atlanta. The tour concludes on Oct. 13 at Fort Worth’s Dickies Arena.

Presale tickets for the Places We’ve Never Been tour will be available starting on Thursday at 12 p.m. local time. General on-sale starts on Friday at 12 p.m. local time. In Colorado, Utah and New York, resale tickets will be available on Stubhub, VividSeats and Ticketmaster, while the remaining states will be only available through Ticketmaster’s Face Value Exchange program. Resale tickets will also be available on Ticketnetwork where fans can get $150 off any $500 order with the code VARIETY150.

The tour announcement comes on the heels of his first stadium show at Los Angeles’ Memorial Coliseum, plus a sold-out co-headlining show with Skrillex in San Francisco and three sold-out nights at Stanford’s Frost Amphitheater.

Popular on Variety

Last week, Fred Again announced his “intimate” new album “Ten Days” that will be released on September 6. The project, which includes previously released singles “Adore U,” “Ten” and “Places to Be” featuring Anderson .Paak and Chika, will have guest appearances from Skrillex, Four Tet, Emmylou Harris, the Japanese House, Obongjayar, Jozzy, Jim Legxacy, Sampha, Soak, Joy Anonymous and Scott Hardkiss.
